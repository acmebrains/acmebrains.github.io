Effective Date: July 20, 2025

ACME Brains, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. This includes rights for California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. What We Collect

We only collect information that you choose to give us, which includes your name and email address (if you submit them through a form on the site). We do not sell your data, and we do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We will only use your information to contact you about beta opportunities, to provide updates about our company, and to improve our internal understanding of interest in our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We will not sell or rent your information. We will only share it with service providers (like email platforms) that help us operate the site or contact you, and if required by law or legal process.

4. Your Rights Under the CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • Access the personal information we have about you.
  • Delete your personal information (with some exceptions).
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell it, but this is your right under the law).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at legal@acmebrains.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

5. Security

We will use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure and, by using the site, you understand and accept this risk.

6. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make big changes, we'll post a clear notice on the site.
